A display campaign on Google Ads using a custom intent audience. With a custom intent audience, you'll have to insert keywords and URLs related to the website - or its product - that you mentioned. Custom intent looks for users who are researching or browsing pages related to the terms and websites you put in.

Results vary but this is as close as it gets. Test with a small budget and scale up if it's successful.
