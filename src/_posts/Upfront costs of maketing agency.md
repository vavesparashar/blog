**Q**: It is often debated that it costs little to no money to start an agency. But  there is a lot that goes into it. So, What upfront costs of marketing agency that a lot of people not considering? 

**A**: It is inexpensive. Especially if you know about SaaS products and have a small budget.

**Starting one is easy. Getting a reputation is not.**

Agencies can easily white label damn near everything. **The issue isn't getting the client, it's keeping the client.**

If you have an agency, your niche, is your life. You can't be all things to all businesses all the time. What you are good at, stay good at.

*Want 100k per year? $1667 per month Revenue x 5 clients. Meaning that is your charge. Client spend is above that.*

Speaking of which...demonstrate value. **Data is gold. Understanding data is platinum.**

Top level cares about the bottom line. That's it. Nothing else. How much are you making for me? For real: lately?

**Google Analytics is the captain of your ship**. If you don't know where your sales are coming from, I, as a client, don't give a sh*t.

The money is in the damn list. That is all. SEM dudes fail after updates. Fb ladies fail after fb updates. Instagram, ticktock eventually...the algorithm is not your friend. What is? **Build that email list.** Period. Side note: chatbot lists too.

Finally, back to my last point. **You can punch someone's email up and create a custom audience. And then a lookalike.** Fb pretty much cemented this and it was copied by everyone else.

*Other important notes

- Golden rule. Karma.

- People are generally good. Sometimes people suck. Life fucking is. Leave it to that. Be good to people even when they are not good to you. See rule 1.

- Publicly traded companies don't give a sh*t about you. They only care about the investors and what the stock is trading at and what the dividend is. This is the top 5%...remember where you come from.

- Working for yourself is absolutely freeing. 
- Five clients per month? Plan it out. 10 hours per week at each project.  i.e. 2 hours per client per week. Do the math. 

Job vs "career". The amount of free time you will have frees your mind to to be, who u want, when I want, with the financial freedom you want wherever u want, doing what you want for whatever reason you want.
