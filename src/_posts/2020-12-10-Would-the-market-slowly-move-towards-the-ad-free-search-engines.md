**My experience is that maybe 5% of the population (counting high) even know that there are ads in Search engines.**

When working with digital marketing it’s easy to mistake yourself for being part of the audience. Your view of things is definitely not in line with the majority.

I too often make the mistake of thinking that I represent the audience. And this phenomenon is even more prevalent and severe in UX, Design and Front end dev. Most designers use a massive super cool Mac screen or something, while the majority of the audience uses a cheap Android phone. Their flashy designs rarely align with what the audience wants or needs. 

Another point that can be made is, how many people pay for youtube so that they can avoid the ads? I find youtube ads more prominent and irritating and I still won't fork out to rid myself of them. 

As for a search engine that's free and without ads, well, I don't see it getting to the technological heights of Google.
